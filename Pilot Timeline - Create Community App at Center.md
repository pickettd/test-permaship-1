# Detailed Pilot Timeline: Create Community App at Center

**Pilot Duration:** April 2026 – November 2026 (Approximately 8 Months; development begins April 2026, real-user testing July – November 2026)  
**Primary Site:** Center, Hawaii  
**Ticket ID:** c7152c90-314c-4ae4-a1f0-1655958d33db

---

## Overview

This timeline breaks the pilot into six monthly phases, with weekly milestones and key activities for each phase. It is intended to guide the Implementation Team, App Admins, Program Leads, and Engagement Team through the full lifecycle of the pilot.

---

## Phase 1: Development & Pre-Launch Preparation (April – June 2026 — Weeks 1–10)

**Goal:** Complete all development, setup, configuration, training, and onboarding preparation before real-user testing begins in July 2026.

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Activities |
|------|-----------|-------|------------|
| Week 1 (April 2026) | App Configuration Complete | Implementation Team | Configure private instance; apply custom Whole-Company/Center branding; set moderation rules; configure keyword filters for risk-based content; set usage window (7:00 AM – 8:00 PM) |
| Week 1 | Participant Selection Finalized | Center Program Leads | Confirm 20–30 kamaliʻi participants (ages 10–18); confirm 5–10 staff (Admins, facilitators, observers); confirm optional external facilitators |
| Week 1 | Consent & Documentation Ready | Implementation Team | Finalize digital communications consent forms; prepare user guides (print and digital); prepare access codes / unique invite links |
| Week 2 | Admin Training Completed | Implementation Team | Conduct admin training sessions covering: post approval workflow, keyword flag response, incident reporting and escalation, daily monitoring log usage |
| Week 2 | Staff Orientation | Center Program Leads | Introduce staff to roles (Admin, Content Contributor, Pilot Observer); review digital safety protocols; review cultural integrity guidelines |
| Week 2 | Gamification Subgroup Selected | Center Program Leads | Identify 1 kamaliʻi group for controlled gamification trial (badges, points, recognitions) |
| Weeks 3–10 (April – June 2026) | Development & Testing (Internal) | Implementation Team | Complete app development; conduct internal QA testing; refine configurations based on findings; finalize onboarding materials |

---

## Phase 2: Initial Rollout & Onboarding (July 2026 — Weeks 11–12)

**Goal:** Launch the app to participants and complete structured onboarding for all user types.

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Activities |
|------|-----------|-------|------------|
| Week 11 (July 2026) | Kamaliʻi Onboarding Sessions | Center Program Leads | Conduct digital literacy overview; cover app navigation, digital behavior expectations, reporting tools, and privacy policies; distribute access credentials |
| Week 11 | Staff Onboarding Complete | Implementation Team | Confirm all staff have accounts and roles configured; verify Admin alert notifications are active |
| Week 12 | App Live — Real-User Testing Begins | All Roles | Kamaliʻi begin posting and interacting; staff post first announcements; Admins begin daily monitoring log; Implementation Team monitors onboarding metrics |
| Week 12 | Baseline Metrics Captured | Implementation Team | Record initial user counts by type (kamaliʻi, staff); track early login frequency, first posts, and app navigation patterns |

---

## Phase 3: Active Pilot — Summer Programming (July – August 2026 — Weeks 13–16)

**Goal:** Run active pilot alongside summer programs; maintain daily monitoring, safety protocols, and engagement support.

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Activities |
|------|-----------|-------|------------|
| Week 13 (July 2026) | Ongoing Daily Monitoring | App Admins | Log in daily; review new posts/comments, flagged content, user reports, and system analytics; complete daily monitoring log |
| Weeks 13–16 | Weekly Implementation Check-ins | Implementation Team | Conduct weekly virtual walk-throughs with Admins and facilitators; document technical issues and usability challenges; compile weekly analytics |
| Week 14 | Mid-Point Evaluation — Early Check-in | Implementation Team | Conduct mid-pilot surveys and focus groups with kamaliʻi, staff; assess adoption, engagement, safety, and cultural fit against success criteria |
| Week 14 | Keyword Filter Review | Implementation Team + Cultural Staff | Review and refine keyword filter accuracy and sensitivity based on Weeks 12–14 flagged content data |
| Week 15 | Gamification Trial Review | Center Program Leads | Evaluate gamification subgroup data: app usage frequency, positive peer engagement, motivation to complete cultural/educational challenges |
| Week 16 (August 2026) | Summer Programming Closes | Center Program Leads | Document engagement trends and content patterns from summer phase; brief staff on any protocol adjustments before fall resumption |

---

## Phase 4: Mid-Pilot Pause & Refinement (August – September 2026 — Weeks 17–18)

**Goal:** Leverage the pause between summer and fall programming to analyze mid-pilot data, address issues, and adjust configurations.

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Activities |
|------|-----------|-------|------------|
| Week 17 (August 2026) | Mid-Pilot Evaluation Report | Implementation Team | Compile quantitative metrics: users onboarded, active users, posts/comments/reactions, flags, incidents, gamification activity; summarize qualitative feedback themes |
| Week 17 | Staff Debrief Session | Implementation Team + Program Leads | Review: usability challenges, moderation experience, cultural fit feedback, gamification trial results; identify adjustments for fall phase |
| Week 18 (September 2026) | App Refinements Applied | Implementation Team | Implement configuration changes based on mid-pilot findings; update keyword filters; refine Admin workflows if needed; update user guides if applicable |
| Week 18 | Fall Program Preparation | Center Program Leads | Confirm returning participants; onboard any new fall-only kamaliʻi or staff; review updated protocols with all Admins |

---

## Phase 5: Fall Resumption & Continued Pilot (September – October 2026 — Weeks 19–26)

**Goal:** Resume active pilot with fall programs; apply mid-pilot refinements; continue data collection toward final evaluation.

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Activities |
|------|-----------|-------|------------|
| Week 19 (September 2026) | Fall Programs Begin | Center Program Leads | Reactivate app engagement for fall programming; brief returning participants on any protocol changes; complete onboarding for new participants |
| Weeks 19–26 | Ongoing Daily Monitoring | App Admins | Continue daily monitoring log; review posts, flags, and reports; respond to incidents per Incident Response Protocol (within 24 hours) |
| Weeks 19–26 | Weekly Implementation Check-ins | Implementation Team | Continue weekly analytics review; document observations; track progress against success criteria |
| Week 20 | End-of-Summer Engagement Summary | Implementation Team | Finalize summer phase data summary; archive for inclusion in final evaluation report |
| Weeks 22–23 | ʻOhana Engagement Preparation (Phase 2 Preview) | Implementation Team + Engagement Team | Begin planning for potential ʻOhana onboarding; gather ʻOhana feedback on digital communication preferences; assess readiness for Phase 2 |
| Week 24 | Cultural Integrity Mid-Fall Review | Cultural Staff + App Admins | Review content posted in fall phase for alignment with cultural values; consult cultural specialists as needed; adjust content guidelines |
| Week 26 (October 2026) | Pre-Final Check-in | Implementation Team | Review data completeness; confirm all surveys are scheduled; prepare final evaluation instruments |

---

## Phase 6: Final Month & Close-Out (November 2026 — Weeks 27–32)

**Goal:** Complete final user engagement and data collection; conduct end-of-pilot evaluation; prepare final report.

| Week | Milestone | Owner | Activities |
|------|-----------|-------|------------|
| Weeks 27–30 (November 2026) | Final User Engagement Period | All Roles | Continue active app use; Admins maintain daily monitoring; staff encourage sustained participation for final data capture |
| Week 29 | Final Surveys Distributed | Implementation Team | Distribute end-of-pilot surveys to kamaliʻi, staff, and ʻOhana (if applicable); conduct focus groups as needed |
| Week 30 | Data Collection Closes | Implementation Team | Export all system analytics; compile Admin monitoring logs; collect all survey and focus group responses |
| Week 31 | Draft Final Evaluation Report | Implementation Team | Compile executive summary; dashboard of key metrics; thematic summaries from qualitative input; technical issues and resolutions; rollout recommendations; lessons learned for integration with information & constituent management system |
| Week 32 | Final Report Review & Presentation Prep | Implementation Team + Program Leads | Internal review of draft report; redact confidential details; prepare summary for leadership presentations |

## Success Criteria Summary

| Category | Target |
|----------|--------|
| Adoption | ≥ 90% of invited users complete onboarding |
| Engagement | ≥ 60% of users post, comment, or react at least once per week |
| Safety | 100% of flagged content reviewed and resolved within 24 hours |
| User Satisfaction | ≥ 85% report a positive or very positive experience |
| Cultural Fit | Cultural staff affirm app supports protocol, language, and values |
| Scalability Readiness | Admin feedback confirms system can scale with adjustments |
